My Summer as a NASA Intern

Welcome!

Hi! My name is Corin Marasco, and this summer, I'm interning with NASA Goddard's Astrophysics Science Division as an OSTEM intern! I've always dreamed of working at NASA someday, and I'm so excited to be fulfilling that dream through this internship.

The Project

This summer, I will be working on cross-calibrating different X-ray satellites using the quasar 3C273. I will be analyzing data from four major X-ray observatories: NuSTAR, XMM-Newton, Chandra, and Swift. Data about the quasar that was collected by those four telescopes will be analyzed and compared in order to find cross-normalization constants. Those constants can then be used for astronomers and astrophysicists to normalize observational data from those telescopes.
